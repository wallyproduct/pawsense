Why now? The timing is no accident.

Smart cat devices have reached a tipping point. Litter boxes now weigh your cat on every visit. Feeders track every meal. Fountains log daily water intake. The behavioral data was never this rich before.

But the data has stayed trapped inside individual apps — siloed, raw, and uninterpreted. No single product connects the signals and tells you what they mean.

PawSense is that layer. We read your existing devices, learn your cat's personal baseline, and surface the pattern changes that matter — before a vet visit becomes urgent.
